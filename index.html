WELC

World Wide Web (World Wide Web, WWW, W3) refers to a worldwide information space where people can share information through Internet-connected computers. It is often simply called the Web. The term is often synonymous with the Internet, but strictly speaking, it is a different concept. The Web is just one service that works on the Internet, such as e-mail But since 1993, the Web has been an absolute position in the Internet structure. It also refers to a transmission that uses HTTP protocols, hypertext and HTML formats on the Internet to exchange pictures and characters.
